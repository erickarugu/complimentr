 What is Complimentr?

Complimentr is a platform that enables colleagues to compliment each other anonymously.

 Why Complimentr?

Compliments play a major role in keeping us motivated and knowing our efforts are appreciated.

 How does it work?

Sign in using credentials shared by the leader in your team. Once you are logged in, you can post new notes plus view compliments posted in your team-wall by other members.

 Is Complimentr anonymous?

Yes. We do not ask for personal details in any stage.

 Can I delete Compliments?

We do not ask for personal details hence it is impossible to know who posted which compliment. However, A reporting feature is under development.
